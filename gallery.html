GALLERY

Here you'll find a gallery of drones & user-submitted images.

Submitted by 'Simon Peake'.

Wonderful image of Simon Peake and his Vulcan S1000 in the foreground. 6 Rotary blades and a lightweight chassis for maximum flight control. If you're interested, check out VulcanUAV for more information about their extensive range of commerical drones.

Amazon Prime Air Drone.

Great image here of what could be the future of parcel delivery. Amazon are currently awaiting approval from the FAA (Federal Aviation Administration) in order to commericially use their drones for parcel delivery.

Submitted by 'Ming Chao'.

Another user-sub. Thanks to 'Ming Chao' for his image of himself fitting his camera to his brand new DJ1 S1000. 8 Rotary blades attached to carbon fibre arms for an ultra-lightweight chassis and great lift-off. For more information about this drone, check our Prices page.

Mountain Rescue Search Drone.

Here we a fantastic image, courtesy of The Patterdale Moutain Rescue Team! They use a DJ1 S900 + A2 Flight Controller in order to scan the mountains for reported lost travellers. A live video feed is transmited back to the team in their truck.

BMX & Quadrocopter.

Here we have an image of a quadrocopter, meaning 4 rotary blades. In the background you can see username 'bmxtrix' on his bike, filming for an upcoming YouTube compilation of stunts and tricks.
